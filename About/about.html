About Us

We found our roots in the rich culture of small town Davis, CA. Here vibrant agricultural innovations and a passion for sustainability flow into an imaginative food scene.

We strive to unearth the food underground, uproot traditional food systems, brush the dirt off a homegrown dining experience. We want you to be able to go anywhere and be immersed in any community through homemade meals. Whether you’re on a distant adventure, or just traveling across the street, Foodnome is here to bring us together over a dining table.

It’s a fact, food tastes better when it’s shared. Foodnome is about creating community, meeting new people and rethinking food systems to make homemade meals affordable. We’re decentralizing the dated food industry and minimizing carbon footprints so everyone can afford delicious meals - even the planet.
